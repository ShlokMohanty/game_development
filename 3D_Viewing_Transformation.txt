Viewing Transformations :
1) Specification of projection Plane(or view plane).
2) Build view Plane Coordinate system 
3) Cinversion of world coordinates to view plane coordinates .
4) Specification of View Volume .

Specification of projection plane (or view plane)
we specify the projection plane by :
1) A reference Point R0(x0, y0, z0) in world coordinates 
2) A unit normal vector N= n1j+n2j+n3k again in WCS axis .
